About Falala Innovation

Falala Innovation is a startup aimed to automate manual activities that people perform in their day-to-day life. This saves people time, increases their productivity, helps them quickly complete their tasks/activities and helps them do so with minimal-to-no errors. The goal of this company is to build products that help people with busy schedules and active lifestyles achieve their goals efficiently through personalized automated processes. The company aims to build hardware and mechanical solutions that have never been built before by employing cutting-edge and latest engineering technologies.
